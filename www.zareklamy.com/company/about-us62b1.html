About Zareklamy

We think big, start small and move fast.

Our company

From the very beginning our main priority has been to help people worldwide by giving them an opportunity to earn additional income remotely. We believe that people who need extra income should be able to use a product they can trust. Along with our platform for advertisers, we are focused on exchanging tasks between earners and marketers, where earners can build relationships with companies and discover new products.

Following our long-term strategy, we constantly strive to provide as many innovative features as possible. Behind our vision is rising unemployment around the world due to automation that could wipe out hundreds of millions of jobs in the next 10 years. There are many more exciting things to come on our platform in the coming years and we will continue to provide what people need.
