Zareklamy is a global freelance marketplace opportunity for people who want to earn money just by completing simple tasks, from home, on their personal devices. Over 7 million people worldwide have chosen Zareklamy to increase their income. We have successfully provided additional source of funds in almost every place on Earth. With 4 different methods of earning money online and multiple types of activity, Zareklamy provides additional workload to people anytime and anywhere.

In its niche and earning segment, Zareklamy is an absolute leader in the number of monetizations available for earning money. By granting possibility to acquire funds on every device with internet connection, Zareklamy helps all people in reaching their financial goals.

To keep up with our rapid growth in the new markets, Zareklamy is looking for experienced and motivated individuals to join our expanding team.

Our IT Department is growing and seeking for talented Technical Writer, who will develop, gather, and disseminate technical information for Zareklamy documentation. Candidate will be responsible for managing the consistency of technical content and its use across departments including development of our products, marketing, and customer service.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Creating paper-based and digital operating instructions, how-to manuals and assembly instructions to help technical support staff and customers.
  • Determine the needs of users of technical documentation.
  • Use the interactive technologies of the Web and Social Media to blend text, graphics, multidimensional images, sound, and video.
  • Edit material prepared by other writers or staff.
  • Standardize content across platforms and media.

What we expect from you:

  • English language (C1/C2).
  • Experience in managing documentation and reporting in the IT industry.
  • Good knowledge about creating professional, detailed and useful instructions for others to follow.
  • Excellent writing skills to be able to explain technical information clearly.
  • Ability to focus on working with information and processes to keep things arranged in orderly systems.
  • Being systematic and well-organised.
  • Experience in working with Technical Writing Tools.
  • Research and exploration skills.
  • Having Web design basics is appreciated.

We offer:

  • Career in rapidly growing IT.
  • Desired food on order, free coffee and tea.
  • Flexible working day starts between 9 and 12 am with at least 3 working days (Monday - Friday).
  • Comfortable and cozy office near Warsaw Spire.
  • Friendly employees.
  • Corporate education (training, seminars, conferences).
