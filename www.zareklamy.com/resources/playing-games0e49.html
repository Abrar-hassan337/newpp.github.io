Explanation of the method of earning and some advices.

Start making money in just 2 simple steps.

Introduction

This earning method is based on playing mobile games you choose. To use this method of monetization, you just need to install the game you like and complete a specific task in the game to earn points, for example: "Reach a certain character level or unlock a feature". Some tasks take several days to complete, however the more difficult the task, the more you earn. The app tracks your progress over a specific time - depending on the task difficulty. Once you finish the task, your Zareklamy account balance will be automatically charged. Our system precisely selects games that may interest you based on your demographics and interests.

Your salary depends on the selected game difficulty level. For each completed task in the game, you will receive as much as the advertiser will pay for meeting the criteria. The salary of this method of monetization is 1.00 - $100.00 for each completed task in the game.

What I need to do?

Choose the method of earning

  1. Sign in to your Zareklamy account.
  2. Click on the menu bar (which is on the top left) and Earn.
  3. Then click the green Earn button, which is below the "Play games" block.

There is also an information icon in the graphic on the top right. If you click on it, the block will show you additional information about this method of monetization, e.g., salary, what to do, how to earn efficiently. All information provided in this block is also written here, where you are reading this.

Start playing games

After clicking the green Earn button, you will be able to see various games which you can choose to play. On the same page you can see the difficulty of the task, and how many points you can earn. 100 points are equivalent to 1 USD in the currency of your Zareklamy account. You can start playing several games at once, but this can be more difficult than you think if you have more than 3 active tasks.

We may show you games with different categories (in accordance with the Terms of Services). Usually, these are action, sport, strategy, battle royale, adventure, role-playing and racing games.


You should be able to see at least 50 games with different difficulty levels of the tasks so there is plenty to choose from. At least once a day you should check the status of your game task in the Zareklamy application and make sure that the task has not expired. If the in-game task verification team detects that a VPN has been used or some other type of cheating has been committed, the task may be rejected. Any attempted fraud may have a negative impact on your Zareklamy account. The task verification process takes up to 3 business days.

Helpful information

You don't have to worry about your game progress as it saves automatically. You can check the progress of tasks every day in the Zareklamy application - remaining time of the task, completion status and points earned.

Currently, this monetization method is only available in the Zareklamy app on Google Play. In the near future, the games will also be available on the App Store and computers. Follow our social media to stay up to date.
